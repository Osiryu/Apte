Don't be afraid! We are here to help you

All people need help to learn a new language, come to APTE to learn easly the french language. You can do it!

Following a specific test concerning the level of letter writing and the level of dysorthography, a program will be adapted to progress and overcome the gaps. The fundamental principle is the de-dramatization of the situation whatever it is and this requires dialogue and attentive listening to the student. Fun exercises, transposition from oral to written, mnemonics, transposition of the usefulness of reading and spelling well in everyday life ...

N'ayez pas peur! On est là pour vous aider
